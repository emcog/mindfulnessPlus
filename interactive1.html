(Title) Mindfulness of breathing 1 or Concentration

(Series) Bootstrap mindfulness

Difficulty raiting: 1

Instructions

Click on the canvas and breath in, release click and breath out. Repeat.

The short explanation

A concentration breathing tool, the more sense faculties employed in the easier it is to locate the point of focus and subsequently maintain concentration. It also becomes easier to notice and the harder it is to loose focus.

The long explanation

This is theory behind this tool. Try, Explore, Play. Feedback welcome
