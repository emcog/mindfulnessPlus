Negative Charging: pay for a course, get a refund for each class attended.

Learning mindfulness will positively effect your life but it requires a minimum level of commitment. Negatively charged tickets incentivise participation and flip the risk away from the consumer. you.

Sure we don’t make money on these tickets but right now we’re working to build a movement. If your lucky enough to get one of these tickets and enjoy our course or just like how we do things, spread the word and give us a shout on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Emojli or any other network you use.

Not all of our events have these tickets, and their numbers are limited.

Small print! Fee’s may be deducted from your ticket, including booking printing and venue.
